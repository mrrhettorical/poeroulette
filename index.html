Path of Exile Build Roulette

This will roll a number of Keystones, skills, and Ascendancies, and you get to decide how you want to build from that. Will you roll four Keystones and pick two? Roll three and try to build around all of them? Pull one Ascendancy and force yourself to work with something you've never tried before? You decide!

Note: This information is accurate as of February 6, 2025, and the current league is 3.25 - Settlers of Kalguur.
